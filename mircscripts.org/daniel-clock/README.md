Clock! 1.0 by Danie|_

---------------------------



1. STARTING CLOCK!



   To start Clock!, type "/clock!" after loading the script file.







2. UNINSTALLING CLOCK!



   After unloading Clock!, you may type "/unset %c!*" to unset Clock!'s 

variables.







3. CONFIGURING CLOCK!



   To configure Clock!, right-click its window. Here are the popup options:



     Mode:       switches between analog and digital modes;

     ------------

     Compact:    shows/hides a resizable border and the titlebar;

     Desktop:    shows Clock! on desktop or as a MDI-child;

     On top:     sets Clock! as a on-top window (only if Clock! is a desktop 

window)

     ------------

     Pointers:   shows/hides the analog mode's pointers;

     Marks:      shows/hides the analog mode's marks (depending on your 

system, hiding marks may speed up display processing);

     Font:       changes digital mode's font;

     Show date:  shows/hides the current date in digital mode;

     Colors:     changes colors of the Clock!



   Some options do not appear when they are not needed.

   Tip: double-click Clock!'s window to switch the Compact option quickly.





4. CLOSING CLOCK!



   To close Clock!, click on its window and press <Esc>.







5. CONTACTING AUTHOR



   On IRC: Danie|_ @ the BRASnet network (irc.brasnet.org)
